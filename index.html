Sushant Singh Rajput

The man who will always be alive in our Hearts

Here's a time line of Sushant Singh Rajput:

  • 1986 - Born in Maldiha,Bihar
  • 2003 - He had ranked seventh in the DCE Entrance Exam and secured admission in the Bachelor of Engineering (Mechanical Engineering) class in Delhi College of Engineering.
  • 2005- He was chosen to be one of the groups of background dancers at the 51st Filmfare Awards.
  • 2006- He was part of the troupe that went to Australia to perform in the cultural programme at the opening ceremony of the 2006 Commonwealth Games and took a drop from engineering in 3rd year.
  • 2008-His debut show was Star Plus's romantic drama Kis Desh Mein Hai Meraa Dil.
  • 2009-2011-followed by a starring role in Zee TV's soap opera Pavitra Rishta.
  • 2013- his film debut in the buddy drama Kai Po Che!, for which he received a nomination for the Filmfare Award for Best Male Debut.
  • 2013 to 2020:-In between he has done many Bockbuster movies as follows:-
    1. starred in the romantic comedy Shuddh Desi Romance(2013
    2. the titular detective in the action thriller Detective Byomkesh Bakshy! (2015).
    3. highest-grossing releases came with a supporting role in the satire PK (2014)
    4. the titular role in the sports biopic M.S. Dhoni: The Untold Story (2016). his performance in the latter, he received his first nomination for the Filmfare Award for Best Actor.
    5. Rajput went on to star in the commercially successful films Kedarnath (2018) and Chhichhore (2019).
    6. His last film, Dil Bechara (2020), was released posthumously on Hotstar.
  • Rajput died by suicide at his home in Bandra, Mumbai in June 2020, at age 34.
